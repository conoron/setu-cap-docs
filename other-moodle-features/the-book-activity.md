# The Book Activity

The Moodle Book Activity is a multi-page learning resource that organizes text, images, and multimedia into a book-like format with chapters and subchapters. It acts as a clean, easy-to-navigate digital manual or mini-website, preventing your main course page from becoming cluttered.
