# Standard Moodle Assignment

Assignments allow students to submit work to their Lecturer for grading. The work may be text-typed online or uploaded files of any type the teacher’s device can read. Grading may be by simple percentages or custom scales, or more complex rubrics may be used. Students may submit as individuals or in groups.
