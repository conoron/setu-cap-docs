# Can I view my timetable on Moodle?

Timetables are **not** available in Moodle.&#x20;

Student lecture timetables are available online at the [SETU Website Student Hub](https://www.setu.ie/current-students/student-hub)

Staff timetables are available at [https://studentssp.setu.ie/staff/login/login.aspx?app=LECTTABLES](https://studentssp.setu.ie/staff/login/login.aspx?app=LECTTABLES)
