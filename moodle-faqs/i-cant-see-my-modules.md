# I cant see my Modules

The list of modules you have access to each year in Moodle is based on your registration information. Once you have completed the registration process for the current academic year, it will take 24 hours for your list of modules to be automatically updated in Moodle.

If you are missing modules, please let the Moodle helpdesk know by email at **moodle@setu.ie** quoting your **student number**. We will work to fix the issue for you as quickly as possible.
