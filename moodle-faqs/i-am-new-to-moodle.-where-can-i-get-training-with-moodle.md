# I am new to Moodle. Where can I get training with Moodle?

All staff can avail themselves of Moodle training from the [CAP Training Hub](https://moodle.setu.ie/course/view.php?id=172287) page, in there you will find lots of content in relation to the VLE Ecosystem. The [Moodle Feature page](https://moodle.setu.ie/course/view.php?id=194666) is a good place to start if you are new to Moodle.
