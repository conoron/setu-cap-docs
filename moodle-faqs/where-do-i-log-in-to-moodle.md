# Where do I log in to Moodle?

Moodle can be accessed on or off the SETU campuses from any location where there is Internet access at https://moodle.setu.ie/.&#x20;

Please enter your username and password in the boxes provided on the Moodle login page. Your Moodle username and password are the same as your SETU computer login that you use to log on to computers in SETU.&#x20;

For students, that is your studentnumber@setu.ie (i.e. **12345678@setu.ie**)

For staff, that is your firstname.lastname@setu.ie (i.e. **david.evans@setu.ie**)

All WIT staff and registered students can log into Moodle. Please watch this [**short video**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ULXzK5nckuk) for further information. SETU Staff and Students will use the Staff and Students Office 365 button to login.&#x20;

If your Moodle password has expired or you have forgotten your password and would like to reset it, please get in touch with computerservices.wd@setu.ie

**Password Reset Summary**

SETU Staff and Students - email _computerservices.wd@setu.ie_ to have your password reset
