# What is a CRN?

A CRN is a 5-digit number that is assigned to each module/course in Moodle. The CRN is usually a teaching module that is on the Approved Course Schedule for the programme of study.

Moodle will import CRN numbers from the student registration system every day. The CRN number in the student registration system contains student registrations, and students are then enrolled in the corresponding CRN in Moodle as per their registration in the student registration system.
