# Turnitin

Turnitin is a web-based text-matching software system that can assist in determining similarities in your uploaded work and the data that Turnitin has on theior database. Turnitin is used to teach students how to properly acknowledge quotations and reference material. Turnitin provides a similarity report; on that report you will have access to a match breakdown. Turnitin does not interpret the report nor dictate action in response to the report, nor does access to the software, in itself, imply any requirement to act on the report. It also offers a GradeMark function that allows assignments to be marked and commented on online for the lecturer to grade and provide feedback to the students.

**How to student access Turnitin**

The Turnitin software is integrated into Moodle.  As a student, you can only submit your assignment once your lecturer has set up a Turnitin Assignment Submission area within his/her module area in Moodle. Separately, you can check your assignment in Turnitin on the [WIT Libraries page in Moodle](https://moodle.setu.ie/course/section.php?id=71039) under the section entitled Referencing Your Work to get an initial similarity report.

If you experience any problems with submitting assignments to Turnitin, please contact the CAP helpdesk by email at moodle@setu.ie
