# Turnitin Similarity Report Score

An Assignment that receives a high similarity score indicates that the data contained within the assignment is similar to other sources (books, other assignments submitted worldwide, websites, databases, etc) checked by Turnitin.

The University does not have a policy on what an acceptable number is; that is a conversation between the Lecturer and the student for that module of study.
