# What are Information Areas in Moodle

Information Areas in Moodle are non-teaching course areas in Moodle. These areas are used for communication with student groups and generic programme information material, such as student handbooks, past exam papers, etc.

All schools will have a Student Information area; there are some other student information areas as well, like the [**Moodle Student Hub**](https://moodle.setu.ie/course/view.php?id=202712)**,** which acts as a one-stop shop to lots of different student support services both in Moodle and on the SETU website.

The [Library also has an Moodle Information area](https://moodle.setu.ie/course/view.php?id=6937) where students can 'check' their work with a Turnitin Checker activity. There are other Information areas such as the [Student Support - Student Life & Learning](https://moodle.setu.ie/course/view.php?id=11430), [Careers Centre](https://moodle.setu.ie/course/view.php?id=4881), and more.

You can switch your module overview from 'In Progress' (Teaching Modules) to Information areas from your Moodle home page.
