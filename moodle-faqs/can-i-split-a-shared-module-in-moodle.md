# Can I split a shared module in Moodle?

Please note that we are no longer able to split single CRNs into multiple Moodle modules, as this carried the inherent risk of students not being enrolled on their correct modules. Splitting a module introduces a manual process and removes the automation from student enrolments.&#x20;

**Editing lecturers on a shared CRN can either:**&#x20;

a) Request a new CRN from their School office to keep students on different programmes separate in Moodle. This may have already been done during the summer months with Registry. So, it's worth checking in with the School office first. Students will appear on modules in Moodle as and when they are registered in Banner.&#x20;

or&#x20;

b) Create groups within the module in Moodle and make content available to their groups via 'restrict access' settings. More on [how you can manage groups in your Moodle module is available at](https://setu-cap.gitbook.io/moodle-guides/course-management/adding-and-managing-groups.) this link.
