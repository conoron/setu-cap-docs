# Giving Student course access

The students who are registered on Banner for the CRN will automatically be enrolled in the corresponding CRN in Moodle. This is an automated process that runs 3 times daily, Monday to Friday; however, you are still free to manually search for and enroll a Moodle user via the participants option in your module.
